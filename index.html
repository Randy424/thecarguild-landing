We haven't launched yet — here's exactly what we'll be

Most car-sharing platforms take a big, blended cut and bury the rest in fees you can't see. The Car Guild is being built on the opposite idea: a small flat fee, incidental charges handed straight back to hosts, and the freedom to bring your own insurer. Here's our commitment, before we've asked you for a thing.

In plain terms: this is an early-stage project, not a live service. We're gauging interest from LA hosts and drivers while we build. Nothing on this page is a live price or a guaranteed launch date. It's what we're committing to deliver.

What we're committing to

The whole reason to build another car-sharing platform is to do the money differently. So we'll say it out loud, and you can hold us to it.

We will

Charge one small, flat fee

A single modest cut on the rental: the kind you can see and check, not a quarter-or-more blended take. We'll publish the exact number the day we set it.

We will

Hand incidental fees back to hosts

Cleaning, delivery, and similar add-ons are the host's work and the host's money. Our intent is that hosts keep those in full, rather than the platform skimming a share or a processing cut off the top.

We will

Let hosts bring their own insurer

We're designing the platform so a host can use their own qualifying commercial coverage if they prefer, or an option we help arrange. The point is choice and a price you can see, instead of coverage bundled invisibly into a big commission.

We won't

Bury the math or invent surprise fees

Every charge, itemized. No percentage service fee that quietly grows with the trip, no line items that appear only at checkout. If we can't show you where a dollar goes, we won't charge it.

We won't

Pretend we're further along than we are

We won't show fake sign-up counters, invent a launch date we can't hit, or quote coverage we haven't secured. When something isn't decided yet, we'll say so, including on this page.

Your car, your coverage, your call.

Insurance is usually the most opaque part of car sharing, bundled into the platform's cut so you never see what it really costs. We're building it the other way around: as its own visible line, with room for you to choose.

Bring your own insurer. Use qualifying commercial coverage you already trust, if that's your preference.
Or use an option we help arrange, priced transparently and shown as its own line, never hidden in a commission.
Either way, you see the price. Coverage details will be finalized with licensed partners before launch.
